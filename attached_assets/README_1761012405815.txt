How to use the templates
------------------------
- Capture real in-app screenshots from your iPhone simulator or device at the exact sizes.
- Paste them onto the template inside the rounded rectangle.
- Add short captions if you like, but avoid violating Apple's guideline that screenshots must represent the app UI.
